Barn door tracker

Throughout grades 10-12 I was a key member and advisor for FIRST robotics competition team 1374. Over the 3 years, I played a role in designing, prototyping, and building three robots for three different competitions. 1374's alliance came second place at the Georgian College district event 2020, and we won the Team Spirit award at Georgian District event 2020 and the Gracious Professionalism award at the North Bay 2019 district event.



Our first attempt at a wall was less than successful. I needed people to hold it together while I took the photo, it took us over 15 hours to make, and it wasn't very stable.

Our final wall design was much more sturdy and took less than half the time to make, as well as being much less expensive than our original design.

The standard pre-2015 FRC control system was used along with a router for interfacing through a laptop to an xbox controller, one joystick mapped to panning and the other to rotating.
